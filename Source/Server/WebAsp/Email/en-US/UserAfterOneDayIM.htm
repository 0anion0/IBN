In the mean time, here are some tips on using the IBN that you might find helpful...

Drag and Drop File Exchange

You can easily send files to your colleagues by dragging and dropping files from your desktop or explorer onto an IBN person or group in the IBN Desktop Client.

Microsoft Office and IBN Integration

If you use Microsoft Word, Powerpoint, Excel, or Project, dont forget that you can download our Office Plug-In Wizard which can be found in the download area of your portal.

Changing Project Task Views

If youre managing a project, dont forget that you can switch views in the task area of the project to look at it from different perspectives including, list view, progress view, and Gantt view.

Receiving Automatic Notifications for Calendar Entries

The IBN supports many different types of calendar views and it also can enable you to enter recurring entries that will automatically notify you and other people connected to these entries.

Adding New Versions to a File in the Library
