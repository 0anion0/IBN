In the mean time, here are some tips on using the IBN that you might find helpful...


Drag and Drop File Exchange

You can easily send files to your colleagues by dragging and dropping files from your desktop or explorer onto an IBN person or group in the IBN Desktop Client contact group. This is a fast and effective way to exchange and share files.


Microsoft Office and IBN Integration

If you use Microsoft Word, Powerpoint, Excel, or Project, don't forget that you can download our IBN Toolbox and Office Plug-In Wizards which can be found in the download area of your portal.  These allow you to work and share information with your business relationships right from your desktop or office applications with ease.


Changing Project Task Views

If you are managing a project, dont forget that you can switch views under the 'Activities' tab to look at it from different perspectives including, list view, progress view, resource view, and Gantt view.


Adjusting Automatic IBN Notifications

As you may already know the IBN has built in notifications and awareness tools that automatically notify you via instant messaging or e-mail.  Did you know that you can customize these notifications by going to the 'Workspace' tab in the portal and selecting the 'Customize' tab.  The batching functionality is great because it will collate all of your events into a single e-mail or collection of notifications.

Use the IBN Incidents/Issues Tracking to Create a Help Desk

Customize the default IBN dictionaries to categorize the way you track business issues, bugs or other types.  Use the automatic help desk assignment capability to route incoming issues to the proper people or projects that they relate to.
